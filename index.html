About

Hi! My name's Conor, and I'm an aspiring software developer from Wellington. My background is in research physics and intellectual property law. I'm most satisfied at work when I'm collaborating with other people to help solve technical problems.

Outside of work, I'm interested in vintage electronics (especially valve-based electronics), running, and mushroom hunting. I have lots of different hobbies and find there's never enough time to pursue all of my interests!

I've also been lucky enough to travel internationally several times over the past few years. I'm happiest when I'm experiencing new cultures and exploring new places, and I want to see as much of the world as I can.
